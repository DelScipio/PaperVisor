## 2024-05-24 - Accessibility on Top Navigation\n**Learning:** Icon-only navigation buttons in NiceGUI often lack basic ARIA attributes by default, impacting accessibility. Adding `.tooltip()` and `.props('aria-label="..."')` is standard procedure to ensure usability for screen readers.\n**Action:** Always verify that every `ui.button(icon=...)` without a label includes both an `aria-label` and a `.tooltip()` in future UI development.
